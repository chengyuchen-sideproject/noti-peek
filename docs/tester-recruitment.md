## English

Indie dev here, doing the 12-testers / 14-days closed test for a new Play account.
**I'll test yours back — drop your opt-in link and I'll join today.**

**The problem:** on Android you can't preview a LINE message — the moment you tap
it, it's marked **read**. And when there's a time-limited reply, that quietly
pressures you into replying right now.

**NotiPeek** fixes that. It captures notifications into a private, on-device
history, so you can **read a LINE (or any) message while it stays unread in LINE**
— no read receipt, no pressure. Reply on your own schedule.

- 👀 Read messages without triggering "read" in LINE
- 🔔 Keeps a local history so you never lose a dismissed notification
- ✅ Choose which apps to capture — pick from your installed apps
- 🔒 No INTERNET permission, no servers, no accounts, no ads. Everything stays on your device.
- 📱 Android 8.0+
