# Privacy Policy — NotiPeek

_Last updated: 2026-07-24_

English | 繁體中文在下方 ↓

NotiPeek is a personal, on-device notification-history app. This policy explains
exactly what it does with your data. The short version: **nothing leaves your
phone.**

## What data the app handles

- **Notification content.** When you grant notification access, Android delivers
  a copy of posted notifications to NotiPeek. The app stores the text (message
  body, sender/title, app name, timestamp) in a local database on your device so
  you can review it as a history.
- That is the only data the app records.

## What the app does NOT do

- **No internet.** The app declares **no `INTERNET` permission**. It is
  technically incapable of sending your data anywhere.
- **No servers, no cloud, no accounts.** Nothing is uploaded, synced, or backed
  up off the device.
- **No analytics, no advertising, no tracking, no third-party SDKs.**
- **No selling or sharing** of any data with anyone.

## Where data is stored & how long

All captured notifications are stored only in NotiPeek's private local database
on your device. They remain until you clear them in the app (per-app or all) or
uninstall the app, which deletes everything. Moving to a new phone starts fresh.

## Permissions used

- **Notification access (`BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE`)** — the core
  permission; it is how the app receives notifications to store locally.
- **Query installed apps (`<queries>` for launchable apps)** — used only to list
  your apps in the capture-filter picker. This is not the sensitive
  `QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES` permission and reveals only apps with a launcher icon; the
  list never leaves the device.

## Intended use

NotiPeek is for reading **your own notifications on your own device**. Do not use
it to access another person's private messages without their consent.

## Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no personal data online.

## Changes

Any future change to this policy will be published at this URL with an updated
date.

## Contact

Questions: open an issue at
<https://github.com/chengyuchen-sideproject/noti-peek/issues>.

# 隱私權政策 — NotiPeek

_最後更新:2026-07-24_

NotiPeek 是一款**本地、裝置內的個人通知歷史 App**。本政策說明它如何處理你的資料。
一句話:**所有資料都不離開你的手機。**

## App 會處理的資料

- **通知內容**:當你授予通知存取權後,Android 會把已張貼的通知副本交給 NotiPeek。
  App 會把文字(訊息內容、寄件者/標題、App 名稱、時間)存進**裝置內的本地資料庫**,
  讓你可以當作歷史回顧。
- 這是 App 唯一會記錄的資料。

## App 不會做的事

- **無網路**:App **未宣告 `INTERNET` 權限**,技術上根本無法把資料傳到任何地方。
- **無伺服器、無雲端、無帳號**:不上傳、不同步、不做裝置外備份。
- **無分析、無廣告、無追蹤、無第三方 SDK。**
- **不販售、不分享**任何資料給任何人。

## 資料儲存位置與保留時間

所有擷取到的通知只存在 NotiPeek 於裝置內的私有本地資料庫。它們會保留到你在 App 內
清除(單一 App 或全部)或解除安裝 App(會刪除全部)為止。換新手機即重新開始。

## 使用的權限

- **通知存取(`BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE`)**:核心權限,是 App 接收通知
  並存到本地的方式。
- **查詢已安裝 App(`<queries>` 可啟動 App)**:僅用於在過濾挑選畫面列出你的 App。
  這**不是**敏感的 `QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES` 權限,只看得到有桌面圖示的 App,清單也不離開裝置。

## 適用用途

NotiPeek 用於閱讀**你自己裝置上、你自己的通知**。請勿在未經他人同意下用於存取他人私訊。

## 兒童

本 App 非針對兒童設計,且不在線上收集任何個人資料。
